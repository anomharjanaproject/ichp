Our Mandate

A Unified Approach to Climate and Child Health

The ICHP provides an essential data-driven framework for national and sub-national authorities to anticipate, monitor, and respond to health threats amplified by climate change, specifically targeting the vulnerability of children.

Multi-Sectoral Data Fusion

Integrating climate projections, environmental data (air quality, hydrology), and localized pediatric health records.

Early Warning Systems

Real-time risk modeling for climate-sensitive diseases like Dengue, Malaria, and heat stress in vulnerable child populations.

Actionable Insights

Converting complex risk data into clear recommendations for targeted public health interventions and climate adaptation policy.
